# Health Insurance 101

Take control of your insurance purchase by knowing what you are buying.

Shopping for a medical plan can be confusing. There are many terms used in health insurance that can be unfamiliar. This guide aims to clarify those terms so you can make informed plan selections.

A downloadable PDF guide ("bbEmployeeShoppingGuide") is available as the primary educational resource for understanding insurance terminology and plan features.

Source: https://knowledge.benefitbay.com/employee/health-insurance-terms-you-need-to-know
