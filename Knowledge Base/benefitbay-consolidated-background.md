## The ICHRA Opportunity

### What is ICHRA?

Individual Coverage Health Reimbursement Arrangement (pronounced IK-RAH) is a benefit product stemming from legislation passed in 2019.

### Market Growth Trajectory

- Fastest-growing form of employer health benefits in the US
- Marking the beginning of the "Personalized Benefits" movement
- Experts predict ICHRA will overtake group benefits similar to how 401(k)s replaced pensions

### Benefits for Employers

- Removes pressure of finding a one-size-fits-all group plan
- Fixed, predictable, and often lower cost of providing health benefits
- Reduced administrative burden
- Financial stability vs. volatile group plan markets

### Benefits for Employees

- Individual health plans matching specific needs
- Same financial support as group plan but with personal choice
- Visibility into government subsidy opportunities
- Ability to choose plans that fit their unique circumstances
