# Verifying Your Personal and Employment Information

## Overview

To begin your ICHRA enrollment journey with benefitbay, you'll need to verify your personal and employment information through a multi-step process.

## Verification Steps

### Step 1: Initial Verification

Upon first login, you'll see a prompt to verify your information. Click the blue "Verify my information" button to start. A progress bar at the top tracks your advancement through the enrollment stages.

### Step 2: Personal Contact Information

Review and verify your personal details on the form. You can make edits if needed. The system requires you to provide a personal phone number before proceeding. Home address changes can be made at this stage but may also be updated later. Select "Continue" to advance.

### Step 3: Employment Information Verification

You'll be asked to confirm your employment details. If you need to make any changes, you will need to contact your Human Resources Department. Once verified, click "Confirm."

### Step 4: Address Review

You have the opportunity to update your home address if necessary. Note that your zip code and county information helps determine which health plans are available to you. Click "Continue" when finished.

## Next Steps

After completing this verification stage, you'll move forward in your ICHRA enrollment journey. The next step is adding family members.
