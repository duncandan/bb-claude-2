# How to Add Dependents to Your benefitbay® Profile

To shop for plans that include your dependents, you must first add their information to the platform.

## Adding Dependents

You can add dependent information to your profile. This step is optional—you may skip it if you prefer not to include family members.

To add dependents, gather their personal details including:
- Date of birth
- Social security number

### Steps to Add a Dependent

1. Click the blue "Add new family member" button on the Employee Info screen

2. Complete the dependent's information form with all required details

3. Click the blue "Save changes" button to confirm

4. To add additional family members, click "Add a family member" again

5. When finished adding dependents, click "Confirm and continue" to proceed
