## Competitor Profiles

### 1. SureCo

**Market Positioning:** "The only administrator 100% dedicated to ICHRA for large groups"

#### Key Differentiators

- **Specialization:** Large-group ICHRA only (100+ employees) — deliberate focus unlike generalists
- **Target:** 8 of top 10 brokerage firms trust SureCo
- **Technology:** Centralized enrollment platform, HRIS integrations
- **Service:** Award-winning, fully managed Open Enrollment with U.S.-based support
- **Unique Value:** Direct carrier payments (no reimbursements required)
- **Expertise:** Founded by brokers and ICHRA pioneers with 60+ years combined experience
- **Compensation:** PEPM-based fees designed to keep brokers whole

#### 2025 Content

- "2025 State of ICHRA Report" - Market trends and insights
- "A New Era of Employee Benefits Takes Hold" (Feb 10, 2025) - In 2024, 70% of brokers said they'd recommend ICHRA, but only 19% were "very likely" to
- "Emerging Data Shaping the Most Impactful Benefits" - Client case study: switched from facing 20% premium increase to 8% decrease with SureCo

#### Competitive vs. BenefitBay

| Aspect | Assessment |
|--------|------------|
| ✅ Advantage | Large-group expertise and relationships (8 of top 10 brokerages) |
| ❌ Disadvantage | Limited to large groups; BenefitBay serves all sizes, especially SMBs |
| Technology | Both real-time, but SureCo emphasizes "smart technology" for ease of use |

### 2. Take Command Health

**Market Positioning:** "Affordable HRA administration pricing for QSEHRA and ICHRA"

#### Key Differentiators

- **Scope:** Dual-product platform (QSEHRA + ICHRA) — broader offering than ICHRA-only competitors
- **Target:** Employers of any size as flexible group plan alternative or supplement
- **Use Case:** Facing high renewals and premium hikes
- **Value Prop:** Flexible group plan alternative
- **Pricing:** Affordable administration (emphasized)

#### Recent Activity

- Appears to have limited public 2024-2025 media presence compared to competitors
- Focus on internal marketing and sales rather than thought leadership content

#### Competitive vs. BenefitBay

| Aspect | Assessment |

| ❌ Disadvantage | Lower visibility in market; less public thought leadership |

## Content Strategy Recommendations for BenefitBay

Based on competitive analysis, BenefitBay should emphasize:

1. **Real-time Advantage** - Highlight speed vs. competitors' slow proposals
2. **Broker Economics** - Emphasize full sales suite and competitive PEPM benefits vs. software-only competitors
3. **Market Momentum** - Continue "ICHRA Fever" narrative (highest media momentum)
4. **Technology Leadership** - Counter Zorro's AI-first with BenefitBay's combined AI + real-time modeling
5. **Cost Control** - Differentiate from Thatch's wellness focus with budget predictability and contribution modeling
6. **All-Size Approach** - Position as "for every employer" vs. SureCo/Zorro's large-group focus
7. **Thought Leadership** - Match Remodel Health's educational content (ICHRA Academy, Exclusive Podcast)
